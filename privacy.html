Data Protection

Privacy Policy

Your creative data is your own. We prioritize the security of your fictional universes as much as your personal information.

1. General Information

This policy outlines how Svitobudova Digital Solutions GmbH handles data collected via this website. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Collection (Mocked for Demo)

For the purposes of this shop demonstration, we mock the following processes:

  • Account Creation: Name and email addresses are stored locally to simulate the user experience.
  • Usage Analytics: We use mock tools to simulate tracking of features like the "Causality Mapper."

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your worldbuilding preferences. You can manage these via the banner on the homepage.

Note: Since this is an academic project, no real personal data is being sold or transferred to third parties.
