Our Mission

The Architecture of Imagination

Founded in Vienna in 2021, Svitobudova was born from a frustration shared by every epic fantasy author, TTRPG game master, and narrative game designer: the tools for writing didn't scale with the complexity of the worlds being written.

We set out to build a professional-grade suite that treats a fictional universe with the same rigor that a software architect treats a system - with dependency graphs, rule validators, and relational data structures.

Today, our 12-person team of developers, UX designers, narrative consultants, and legal professionals operates from our headquarters in Vienna's 7th district, serving thousands of creators across Europe and beyond.

Our Brand Identities

Three distinct brand forms protecting the Svitobudova identity. All marks are pending registration with the EUIPO under the relevant Nice Classification classes.

Brand Form 1 - Figurative Mark (Bildmarke)

✦ The Architect's Star

The Architect's Star

An eight-pointed stellar form, representing the intersection of infinite narrative branches — each ray a story path converging at the world's core. The form is derived from the geometric hexagonal cell (⬡) common in worldbuilding map conventions.

Colors: Gold (#C9A84C) on void black (#09090F). No similar marks found in EUIPO eSearch for Class 42 software services.

Nice Class 42 - Software as a Service

Brand Form 2 - Shape Mark (Formmarke)

The Nested Worlds

Three concentric hexagons, each representing a layer of narrative depth: the world's physical laws, its history, and its living characters. The central point is the author - the architect at the center of all creation. This three-dimensional shape is unique and non-descriptive for software services.

Colors: Gold on black only. No decorative variation to maintain distinctiveness.

Nice Class 42 · Class 9 - Software

Brand Form 3 - Abstract Pattern Mark (Abstraktes Markenzeichen)

The Constellation Grid

An abstract radial dot pattern inspired by star charts and narrative node graphs. Denser at the centre and diffusing outward, it symbolises a world of detail radiating from a single authorial origin point. When used, it appears as a background texture or watermark - never as a standalone decorative element.

Colors: Gold only. The arrangement and radial fade pattern are the registrable element, not the color alone.

Nice Class 42 · Class 41 - Education & Training

Trademark Compliance Statement

All three brand forms have been searched against the EUIPO eSearch Plus database, TMview, and the Madrid Monitor prior to adoption. The name "Svitobudova" is a Ukrainian-derived neologism with no prior registration in the software, creative tools, or publishing sectors within the European Union.

  • The gold color (#C9A84C) is not claimed as a standalone trademark - it is only protected in combination with the shapes above.
  • The hexagonal shape is used in a highly specific, multi-layered arrangement not found in existing marks for Class 42.
  • The name deliberately avoids similarity with existing marks such as "Scrivener," "World Anvil," or "Notion."
